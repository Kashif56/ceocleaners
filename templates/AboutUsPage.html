Our Story

The Journey So Far

CleaningBiz AI was founded in 2023 by a team of technology experts and cleaning industry veterans who recognized the need for innovative solutions in the cleaning sector.

What started as a simple idea to improve customer communication has evolved into a comprehensive platform that addresses the unique challenges faced by cleaning businesses.

Today, we're proud to serve cleaning businesses across the country, helping them streamline operations, improve customer satisfaction, and drive growth.
